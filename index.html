Waste Awareness

Learn how to segregate and manage waste responsibly

🌿

Biodegradable

Food scraps, garden waste, paper. Decomposes naturally. Compost it!

  • Vegetable peels
  • Fruit waste
  • Dry leaves
  • Tea bags
♻️

Recyclable

Plastics, glass, metals. Can be reprocessed into new products.

  • Plastic bottles
  • Glass jars
  • Newspapers
  • Cardboard
☠️

Hazardous

Dangerous to health and environment. Requires special disposal.

  • Batteries
  • Paints & solvents
  • Chemicals
  • Medical waste
💻

E-Waste

Electronic devices and components with toxic materials inside.

  • Old phones
  • Laptops & TVs
  • Wires & cables
  • Batteries

🌍 Top 10 Waste Reduction Tips

01

Carry reusable bags and avoid single-use plastics

02

Compost kitchen waste at home using a bin

03

Segregate waste at source – don't mix categories

04

Buy products with minimal or recyclable packaging

05

Donate or repair items instead of discarding them

06

Use both sides of paper before recycling

07

Never burn or bury hazardous waste

08

Return e-waste to authorized collection centers

09

Encourage neighbors and society to report waste

10

Participate in local clean-up drives regularly

About EcoReport

Our Mission

EcoReport is a community-driven waste management platform built to make waste reporting easy, transparent, and rewarding. We connect citizens with waste collection teams and promote responsible disposal practices.
